About me

I am a PhD Student in Secure Reinforcement Learning at the KTH Statistical Learning and Control Group, under the supervision of Prof. Alexandre Proutiere. My research interests include adaptive control, reinforcement learning and security of stochastic dynamical systems.

I am an active member and coordinator of the Statistical Learning Group, which aims at improving the theory of Statistical learning and control theory.

Apart from my academic duties, I also do volunteering. At the moment I’m currently helping to organise THS Armada 2019, and I look forward to new adventures.

I have many interests: volunteering, reading thrillers, traveling, learning new languages, teaching and mentoring, making origami, volleyball/football, watching movies/tv-series/formula 1/moto-gp/, cooking, … the list is very long, but, essentially, I’m very curious!
